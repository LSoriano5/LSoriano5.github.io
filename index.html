About Me

Born and raised in a small town of Ojai,CA I had set my eyes on something big. I grew up wanting to be an engineer, and wanted to pursue Civil engineering right out of high school. But with obstacles and changes, I went on different paths to get where I am today. I pursued becoming a pilot, doctor, physical therapist, and nutritionist. Eventually I graduated from Cal State Channel Islands with a BS in Health Science deciding to become a Physical Therapist. But soon changed my path back to an engineering field because I wanted more. I wanted to be challenged and wanted to let my creative side show in a way that excites me. I want the freedom to explore the world and in a way contribute something into it as well. And so today, I am pursuing a second degree in Computer Science to fullfill that dream and want.
